My Story

I got my start at Webedia Group, creating animated ads for sites like IGN, AdoroCinema, and TudoGostoso. Back then it was all Flash—but I helped lead the shift to CSS and jQuery animations to make things faster and more SEO-friendly. Working with big names like EA, Ubisoft, and movie studios gave me a front-row seat to high-impact digital campaigns. After that, I spent time as a visual designer at an ad agency and then at CNN, where I worked on creative projects and helped keep the site fresh with content, especially during elections. Most recently, I’ve been a web developer for a Korean healthcare company, building their Brazilian site, launching new products, and keeping their blog up to date.
